## 🚀 Getting Started

After installing Friend, you can open the app from your desktop or Start menu.

When you run Friend, it places AI helpers on your desktop. These helpers stay at the bottom of your screen for easy access.

You will see five characters. Each character specializes in a different office task. Clicking a character opens a chat window where you can ask questions or get help.

Friend works quietly in the background, ready to assist when you need it.

---

## 🖥️ How to Run Friend

- Find the Friend icon on your desktop or in your Start menu.

- Double-click the icon to open the app.

- The AI helpers appear at the bottom of your screen.

- Click on any helper to start chatting.

- You can minimize the main window; the helpers remain visible.

- To close the app, right-click its icon in the system tray and select "Exit."

## 💬 Using Friend

Each AI helper has a specialty. You can ask them about different office tasks like document review, scheduling, or general questions.

Here’s what you can do inside the chat window:

- Type your question and press Enter.

- Attach files like images, documents, or notes to get tailored help.

- Start group chats where several helpers discuss your issue.

- Switch between direct API calls or service modes in settings.

## ⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

Friend supports quick keys for better workflow:

- `Ctrl + Alt + F` — Show or hide all AI helpers.

- `Ctrl + Alt + C` — Open chat with your favorite helper.

- `Ctrl + Alt + S` — Open system tray menu.

You can customize shortcuts in settings.
